Quesadilla Recipe

Description

A late night classic. You can't go wrong with a quesadilla

You can make it with just cheese, or you can stuff it with various things

Ingredients

  • Soft tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Optional: stuff with meat or vegetables

Steps

  1. Put shredded cheese into one half of the tortilla
  2. Fold tortilla
  3. Cook tortilla
