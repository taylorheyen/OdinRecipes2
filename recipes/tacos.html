Taco Recipe

Description

Everybody loves tacos, they are a classic

You can have either hard shell or soft shell tacos

Ingredients

  • Hard Shell tortillas (or soft)
  • Ground beef (or other meat)
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Choice of Vegetable Topping

Steps

  1. Cook Ground beef with seasoning
  2. Stuff beef into taco shell
  3. Top with shredded cheese
  4. Top with vegetables of choice
